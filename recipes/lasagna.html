Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 1b ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 jar marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly.
