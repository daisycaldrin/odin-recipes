Description

A creamy, fresh avocado dip with lime, cilantro, and a touch of spice — perfect with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork to your desired consistency.
  3. Add lime juice and salt, and mix well.
  4. Fold in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning, then serve immediately.
