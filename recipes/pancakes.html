Description

Light and fluffy pancakes made from scratch - a classic breakfast favourite that's quick and easy to whip up

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased griddle or pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the griddle, about 1/4 cup per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook until golden brown.
